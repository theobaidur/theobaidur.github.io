Cordes Fellows

Since 2010, our Cordes Fellows program has provided annual scholarships for 50 of the worldâ€™s most promising emerging social entrepreneurs to participate as full Delegates in the Opportunity Collaboration. The Collaboration is a five-day invitation-only global business retreat, held each October in Mexico and co-chaired by Ron and Marty Cordes, which brings together 350 global leaders focused on innovative, sustainable solutions to global poverty.

Cordes Fellows are selected each year through a global selection process. Fellowship applications are accepted beginning on December 1st through January 31st of each year.
