    CC: Pain Management for Stomach Issue

    Vitals: Blood Pressure: 120/80

    S: Patient complained of Stomach Pain. Allergies are acting up. Patient was given a 2 week dose of Xamalticide. The dose was increased last week. Since then the stomach pain has increased. Would like to try Genemicilin instead.

    O: General: In no acute stress. Chest: Lungs clear. No wheezing. Heart: RR. No murmurs. No rubs. No gallops. Abdomen: Soft, no tenderness, no masses, BS normal.

    A:Stable. Needs rest and regular medications dose needs to decrease for the next two weeks. The patient has been improving for the last 3 months at a good pace. Need to still monitor this to ensure that the pace of growth continues. In addition, the patient has had a family history of Stomach Aches. The patient’s lab results for the A1C tests were negative. However, the lab test results for Genemycin came positive. This means that the patient could be having a weak strepthamycin bacteria in the stomach still. This bacteria is not getting caught in the A1C tests, but is getting the energy it needs to be able to show up in the intestines. The patient will be monitored closely

    DIAGNOSES:
    A – Hypertension
    C - Gout

    P: Refill Meds. Monitor pain for the next one week to see if the pain level goes down.

    MEDICATIONS:
    Simvastatin 10 mg tab Once daily
