ABOUT US

WHAT IS MARKETMAN ?

Marketman is a collaboration platform between retailers and their suppliers. The system manages the procurement and supply from product catalog and prices, through the delivery and accounting. The system is built to provide maximum value with minimum effort and no technical knowledge requirement.

Marketman was built out of desire to help businesses streamline processes and save money.

THE PEOPLE BEHIND MARKETMAN

We are software professionals, collaborating with restaurant professionals and wholesale suppliers, in order to make products that really make your life easier. In addition to good products we try our best to listen to our customers and provide them with the best possible service.

But forget about us, you are the one who is really important. Business owner, retailer, wholesaler, we can really make you life easier. You need Marketman so you can focus on your business and what you do best.

THE TEAM

Noam Wolf - CEO
Noam is an accomplished entrepreneur with over 10 years' experience within the web and mobile market.
Noam is also a co-founder of SayWhat, a mobile app which lets users call and text together.
Prior to Marketman, he was the founder and CEO at Mintmark LTD, acquired by Walla.co.il (Bezeq Group), and earlier in his career he held sales and business development roles and was even a C++ programmer for a while.

Igal Wolf - VP R&D
Igal is the brain behind the complexity and simplicity of Marketman platform. He is nothing like those guys who program all day long as he loves talking to customers and actually understanding what they need what they use, which helps him make better products.
