DIGITAL WALLS

This project is a proposal as a part of the Parsons DT + China collaboration in Guangzhou, China.

OVERVIEW

The concept explored is as follows: Walls and borders separate us. Technology has allowed us access beyond borders We seek to create a wall that transcends space and time and can connect instead of seperating. The proposal is a gallery installation that would be installed in two different places in the world. The installation will consist of two rooms with a touch-screen mounted on one wall and a web camera that streams live video to the other location. The touch screen can be touched to turn pixels on or off. Each pixel in the ON state represents a "hole in the wall." Each pixel will be a pixel of footage streaming from the other gallery. As you turn ON more pixels, you make more "holes" that reveal what is going on in the other gallery cube. The gallery holes are mutually connected and the same pixel is turned ON in both galleries, therefore both galleries will be able to see each other once all the pixels are turned on. We believe that this beautiful interaction across the border could simulate the breaking of time and space, pixel by pixel.

ROLE

Concept, UI and UX design.

GONNE WITH THE WIND

Personal project prototype.

OVERVIEW

Gone with the wind is a proposal for an Interactive installation that explores the individual presence and importance in urban context. In big urban cities people come and go, disappearing and merging with the scenario. This project aims to make people aware and reflect upon it. There will be a big screen installed on the city and when the users cross it, they will see their reflex on it as if it was a mirror and their reflex will disappear and merge with the city.
