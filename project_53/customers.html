About Marketman

Marketman is a collaboration platform between retailers and their suppliers. The system manages the procurement and supply from product catalog and prices, through the delivery and accounting. The system is built to provide maximum value with minimum effort and no technical knowledge requirement. Marketman was built out of desire to help businesses streamline processes and save money.
